Instructions
How to get YouTube embed URL:
  1. Go to the YouTube video you want to embed
  2. Click the "Share" button below the video
  3. Click "Embed"
  4. Copy the URL from the src attribute (e.g., https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID)
  5. Paste it in the Hero Video URL field above
Tip: The hero video will be displayed on the main landing page to showcase your treasure hunt adventures.
