Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how SKSC MYSILLYDREAMS PRIVATE LIMITED collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last Updated: January 2025

Company Information

Treasure Hunt Adventures is owned, operated, and organized by SKSC MYSILLYDREAMS PRIVATE LIMITED, a company incorporated under the laws of India. All services, activities, and operations are conducted under the complete ownership and management of SKSC MYSILLYDREAMS PRIVATE LIMITED.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name and contact details
  • Email address and phone number
  • Age and gender information
  • Emergency contact information
  • Medical consent and health information

Technical Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device and operating system details
  • Website usage and navigation patterns
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

SKSC MYSILLYDREAMS PRIVATE LIMITED uses your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Processing registrations and organizing treasure hunt activities
  • Communication: Sending confirmations, updates, and important safety information
  • Safety & Security: Ensuring participant safety and emergency contact procedures
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and maintaining records
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing feedback to enhance our adventure experiences
  • Marketing: Sending promotional materials (with your consent)

Data Protection & Security

SKSC MYSILLYDREAMS PRIVATE LIMITED implements comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

Secure Storage

Personal data is stored on secure servers with restricted access controls.

Access Control

Only authorized personnel have access to personal information on a need-to-know basis.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

Right Description
Access Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
Rectification Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
Erasure Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
Portability Request transfer of your data to another service provider
Objection Object to processing of your personal information for marketing purposes
Restriction Request limitation of processing under certain circumstances

Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized services. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide relevant content and advertisements
  • Ensure website security and functionality

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Services

SKSC MYSILLYDREAMS PRIVATE LIMITED may use third-party services to enhance our operations:

Third-Party Disclosure
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing
  • Email Services: For communication and notifications
  • Analytics Tools: For website performance analysis
  • Cloud Storage: For secure data backup and storage

These third parties are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for the specified purposes.

Data Retention

SKSC MYSILLYDREAMS PRIVATE LIMITED retains personal information for the following periods:

  • Registration Data: 7 years from the date of last activity (for legal compliance)
  • Medical Information: 7 years from the date of collection (for safety records)
  • Communication Records: 3 years from the date of last communication
  • Website Analytics: 2 years from the date of collection
  • Marketing Data: Until consent is withdrawn or 2 years of inactivity
